    • My Projects

    Design and Development of Autonomous Fire Fighting Robot

    Lead a 3-person research team to design a fire-fighting robot that can suppress fires without additional threat to fire-fighters. Designed it in compressed in size to facilitate small location entry for a deeper reach of extinction of fire in a limited space than other conventional robot. It also features an ultrasound sensor that avoids striking any obstructions and objects around, while a fire detector is attached. The outcome was the ability to automatically identify fire places and to remotely extinguish flames at certain distances.

    Bipedal Humanoid Walking Robot – “Lee”.

    ”Lee” is the first bipedal humanoid robot in Bangladesh, an upgraded version of “Ribo”. I created a database for the resources available in the lab. To have an increased conversational time, I analyzed data from a number of users and worked with Bangla AI.

    Autonomus Line Following Robot

    Built a line follower with the most popular control mechanism, PID, and participated with this robot in three contests. I learned a lot of this during this project, such as teamwork, working under pressure, and dealing with failure.
